Final Project

Milestone 3 (of 3)

This milestone represents the finalizing of your project (and video).

Once you submit your project notebook to gradescope, it will be considered ready to be reviewed by your peers and graded by the staff.

Although peer reviews will not start until Thursday, May. 6th, 10:00 AM, the staff may start the grading process as soon as the assignment is submitted.


Before you submit tips:

  • Please review project milestone 0 to ensure you haven't forgotten something.
  • Ask a friend to review your notebook for you. Have them do a dry run and see if they think anything is missing or poorly explained.
  • They should be able to run your notebook without having to log into google or needing to copy it

  • You can assume the reader will use Chrome.


Before you review tips:

  • When you peer review someones work, always reset your expectations. Don't grade your second project based on how well the first project was done.
  • Don't grade someone's project relative to your own project.
  • Don't rush through the project, be mindful of the work required.

Managing Review Issues

Broken Notebooks while doing reviews

If you are unable to access a project or the project just doesn't work:

  • notify the course staff on piazza in a private post with the FULL URL of the project. We can contact the owner and see if the issue can be addressed.

  • in the meantime, continue to next one project in your list

Final Feedback; Stay Anonymized

  • Be sure your project notebook doesn't have any specific identifiers in it if you are concerned about anonymity.

  • As a reviewer, your information will not be revealed as well. In fact, the survey data provides no way to do that.

  • Be professional, and your comments made in the survey will not be filtered for sensitive information if they are given back to the project owner.

  • If time permits, we hope to compile all the feedback for each person and deliver it such that the reviewer will remain anonymized.
