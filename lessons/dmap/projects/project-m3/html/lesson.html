Final Project Milestone 3 (of 3)

This milestone represents the finalizing of your project (and video).

Once you submit your project notebook to gradescope, it should be ready to be reviewed by your peers.


Tips:

  • Please review project milestone 0 to ensure you haven't forgotten something.
  • Ask a friend to review your notebook for you. Have them do a dry run and see if they think something is missing.

  • They should be able to run your notebook without having to log into google or needing to copy it (but they should use chrome).


Lesson Assignment

This milestone has TWO deliverables:

Project Notebook

You will upload to gradescope your project notebook (not this one). Download your project as a .ipynb file. That is the file you will upload to gradescope to get credit for this milestone.

Peer URLS

When you submit your project file, you will be issued at least 4 URLS to grade. Each URL will point to the survey you will use to grade the project. The survey used to collect your feedback will not open until the specified date given on Piazza.

If you are given more than 4 URLS to grade, you can grade them in the order given. You're welcome to do any additional ones, but we expect to do the first four.

Broken Notebooks while doing reviews

If you are unable to access a project or the project just doesn't work:

  • notify the course staff on piazza in a private post with the FULL URL of the project. We can contact the owner and see if the issue can be addressed.
  • continue to next one project in your list
