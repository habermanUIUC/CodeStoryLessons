Currency and Unit Conversion

This coding challenge is about converting currency and units.

Lesson Assignment

Write a function named unit_converter which has three parameters:

  • quantity, total, convert_to (in that order)

The function unit_converter converts the price paid for a specific volume amount from one currency into a different price/unit.

For example, let's say you are in Toronto and you pay C$124.00 for 61 liters of gas (i.e. you paid 124.00 Canadian dollars (CAD) for 61 liters of gas). How much is that when expressed in USD per gallon? That is, what's the price per gallon in US dollars?

Also, let's say you are in Champaign and you pay $47.00 for 16 gallons of gas. How much is that expressed in CAD per liter?

You can assume there are only two currencies:

  • USD ($ US dollars)
  • CAD (C$ Canadian dollars)

If convert_to is '$' the total is in CAD and the units are in liters.

If convert_to is 'C$', the total is in US dollars and the units are in gallons.

Your function should work like this:

Example 1

Paid C$124.00 (CAD) for 61 liters

How much is that per gallon (USD)?

USD_per_gallon = unit_converter(61, 124.00, '$')

print('$', USD_per_gallon , "per gallon")

Example 2

Paid $47.00 (USD) for 16 gallons

How much is that per liter (CAD)?

print('C$', unit_converter(16, 47.00, 'C$'), "per liter")

constants

Here are the relevant constants: (note that they are inverses, only really need 2)

quantity

LITERS_PER_GALLON = 3.78541
GALLONS_PER_LITER = 0.264172

exchange rates

CAD_TO_USD = 0.76
USD_TO_CAD = 1.31

A few restrictions:

  • you can ONLY use one return statement in your function.
  • you cannot use the keywords: and, or
  • return None, if division by zero is an issue.

Food for thought.

The current gas price in Vancouver BC is C$1.33 a liter. The current gas price in Champaign, IL is $2.42 a gallon. How can you use your function to determine how much more (or less) gas prices are in Champaign?

Your answer could be either in per gallons, per liters or even expressed as a percentage.

How many calls to the unit_converter function would you need to make to answer the question?
